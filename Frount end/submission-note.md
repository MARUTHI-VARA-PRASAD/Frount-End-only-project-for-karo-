# Karo Pitch Demo Submission Note

**Tool used:** ChatGPT-assisted HTML, CSS, and JavaScript build.

**Why this design:** I designed the page to feel investor-facing and premium rather than like a generic startup template. The layout combines strong founder-focused messaging with a structured flow: hero, platform purpose, process, founder categories, investor intent, startup discovery, and final CTA. Visually, I used a dark editorial background, warm signal colors, layered glassmorphism cards, and motion effects to make the concept feel modern, credible, and high attention on both desktop and mobile.

**Project intent:** The homepage is positioned to show Karo Pitch as a Bharat-first startup discovery and pitch platform that can help early-stage founders gain visibility, investor access, and funding opportunities.
