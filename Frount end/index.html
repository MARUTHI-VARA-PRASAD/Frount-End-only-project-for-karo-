About Karo Pitch

The investor access layer for high-potential founders across Bharat.

Thousands of founders are building meaningful businesses, but many still lack direct access to investors, structured mentorship, and visibility.

Karo Pitch was created to close that gap through curated pitch events and a discovery platform designed to help promising startups get seen, evaluated, and backed.

The mission is simple: make startup funding more accessible for founders from Tier-2, Tier-3 cities and Bharat-first markets who are already building real companies.

How It Works

Simple pipeline. Serious outcomes.
